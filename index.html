Yu Guo

I am a PhD Student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen), and Prof. Shunbo Lei is my supervisor.

I received the Master degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, and Prof. Huijin Fan was my supervisor, and Bachelor degree from China Three Gorges University.

My research direction is the distributed algorithm and its application in future energy system.
